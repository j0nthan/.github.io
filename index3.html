Tips for Time Management

One tip from the bunch to start your time management journey is to first and foremost create a Todo list.
This Todo list can be from being on a piece of paper or a word document on your laptop. Using a Todo list
helps you manage time before you start doing the activity, this allows you not to get carried away doing one
activity for hours. Which could lead you to not find time to complete the other activities you also had to finish
on the same day.
