About Time Management?

Time management is when you effectively plan your time for each specific activity you have planned for
that period of time. Many successful people use time management to use their time wisely to complete
their tasks so they don't have to work smarter, not harder, meaning that they can produce quality work
instead of rushing to not miss a deadline, meaning you stressed and producing work that is not as good
as what you could've produced if you had time and managed your time wisely.
